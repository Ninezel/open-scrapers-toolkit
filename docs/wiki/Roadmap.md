# Roadmap

Open Scrapers Toolkit is growing into a reusable scraping library, not just a terminal tool.

## Next priorities

- add another 10 to 15 public-interest sources
- deepen official health, climate, disaster, and policy coverage
- add cache and retry helpers for app and bot integrations
- add publisher helpers for Discord, webhooks, and snapshot outputs
- improve health alerting and live-source monitoring
- grow contributor templates, fixtures, and troubleshooting notes

## Target source areas

- WHO
- UN News
- OECD
- humanitarian updates
- more hazard datasets
- more academic and journal feeds

## Companion alignment

The desktop repo should keep tracking toolkit capabilities so GUI users and bot developers can rely on the same scraper ecosystem.
